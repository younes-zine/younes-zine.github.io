Younes Zine

About Me

I am a postdoc in the group of Martin Hairer at EPFL (Switzerland). I was previously a Ph.D. student at the University of Edinburgh (UK) under the supervision of Tadahiro Oh.

Research interest

  • Nonlinear dispersive and parabolic PDEs
  • Stochastic PDEs, Probability theory
