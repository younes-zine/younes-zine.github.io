About Me

I am a postdoc in the group of Prof. Martin Hairer at EPFL (Switzerland). I was previously a Ph.D. student at the University of Edinburgh (UK) under the supervision of Prof. Tadahiro Oh.

Research interest

  • Nonlinear dispersive and parabolic PDEs
  • Stochastic PDEs, Probability theory

    • Papers

    8. Learning with minibatch Wasserstein: asymptotic and gradient properties (with K. Fatras, R. Flamary, R. Gribonval and N. Courty) Proceedings of the 23nd International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Statistics. (2020) (arXiv link)
